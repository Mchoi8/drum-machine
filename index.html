User's Manual

1) Users are able to choose from three different drum sounds: the hi-hat, drum snare, and bass kick. By pressing the 'q', 'w', and 'e' keys the drum sounds will trigger and play the audio. Users can then play their own drum beats using the keys.

2) The keys for the bass start from 'i', 'o', 'p' which also follows the format for the default and different designated buttons. This allows the user to play the drum and the bass sounds with their left and right hand.

3) To start, click on the "Default Sounds" button to hear the basic drum and bass sounds. Users have the choice to listen to three different types of sounds playing (hihat, snare, kick) by simpling clicking on the Sounds button and pressing the same keys.
